Samsung Solve for Tomorrow Entry Submission

To enter Solve for Tomorrow:

  • Complete the Solve for Tomorrow application form, and
  • Upload your entry using one of the approved entry formats:
    • Written plan or essay (max 1,000 words)
    • Poster, diagram, model, or artwork (submitted via PPT, PDF, video or jpg format)
    • Presentation PowerPoint (max 10 slides)
    • Video presentation (max 5 minutes)

Some final tips for submitting your idea:

  • Final working prototypes are not required. However, we encourage you to outline or expand on how your idea could be developed further
  • Use simple, conversational language to explain your idea to the judges
  • Please read the terms and conditions in detail before submitting your idea
  • Entries close at 11.59pm on DD MM YY. If you require additional support to complete your application form and idea submission, please email the Samsung team on support@solvefortomorrow.ws
